Weather-Informed Dynamic Pricing for MyMovies.Africa™

Capstone group project under Moringa School | Data-driven demand forecasting

Contributed to Yakwetu, a capstone group project developed under Moringa School for MyMovies.Africa™, aiming to optimize content pricing using data on user behavior and external factors like weather.

I supported the development of predictive models and helped structure data workflows using Python and Pandas. The insights were used to design a dynamic pricing model that adjusts content prices based on forecasted demand signals.

This project demonstrates my ability to work in a team, apply analytical thinking, and contribute to scalable solutions at the intersection of data science and business strategy.
